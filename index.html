COLOURS OF NATURE

Granite is a type of igneous rock that is composed primarily of quartz , feldspar and mica . It is formed through the slow cooling and solidification of molten magma beneath the Earth surface . The mineral composition of granite can very depending on the specific loction where it is Mined . Their natural and original textures give the rooms an uniqueness and elegance. Its outstanding Hardness , Versatility , Durability and Resistance to abrasion make this the perfect material for outdoor and indoor use.
